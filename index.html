About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the department of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Auburn Univiersity, under the supervison of Dr. Sathyanarayanan N. Aakur . I have completed my Bachelors degree in Electronincs and Communication Engineering from KUET, Bangladesh.

Currently, I am working in the group of Open-ended Reasoning and Knowledge Acquisition (ORCA) Lab led by Dr. Aakur. My primary research interest is video event Understanding using structured graphical representation and common-sense reasoning. I have worked on scene graph generation using generative graph transformer, open world event understanding in ego-centric videos using neuoro-symbolic frameworks and probablistic search aprroaches. I am also working on procedure understaning in long multi-view procedural ego-centric videos. Apart from scene and video understanding, I am also interested in generative AI, specially in video and image generation using diffusion models. I am also interested in building solution for Real-World problems specially in the fields of Agriculture, marine life, eco-logical data, medical imaging etc. If you are interested in colaboration in any of the areas, please feel free to reach out at sanjoykundu.ece@gmail.com.
