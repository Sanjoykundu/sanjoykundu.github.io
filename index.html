About Me

I have joined the computer science department at OSU as a PhD student in Spring 2020 under the supervison of Dr. Sathyanarayanan N. Aakur . I have completed my Bachelors degree in Electronincs and Communication Engineering from KUET, Bangladesh.

Currently, I am working in the group of Computer Vision and Understanding Lab led by Dr. Aakur. My primary research interest is video event Understanding using self-supervised predictive learning and common-sense reasoning. I am also working on scene graph generation using generative transformer. We have a plan to extend the rich structural information of scene graphs for video event understanding.
